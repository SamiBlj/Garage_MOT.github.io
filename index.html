Interactive MOT Info

Everything you need to know about MOT testing, requirements, and what to expect.

An MOT (Ministry of Transport) test is a mandatory annual safety and emissions check for vehicles over 3 years old. It ensures your car meets legal safety standards and doesn't harm the environment.
MOTs are legally required for road safety. Without a valid MOT, you could face fines up to £1,000, vehicle seizure, or invalid insurance. More importantly, it prevents accidents and ensures your vehicle is roadworthy.
Brakes, steering, suspension, tyres, lights, exhaust emissions, seatbelts, windscreen, wipers, horn, and general vehicle condition. We also check for any modifications that might affect safety.
Standard MOT test costs £54.85 (as of 2024). Additional fees may apply for repairs needed to pass. We offer competitive pricing and transparent quotes for any required work.
A standard MOT takes about 45-60 minutes. We'll provide a courtesy car if available, or you can wait in our comfortable waiting area with free WiFi and refreshments.
If your vehicle fails, we'll provide a detailed report of required repairs. You have up to 10 working days to fix the issues and get a free retest. We can perform all repairs on-site with guaranteed workmanship.
MOTs are due annually. Check your V5C registration certificate or contact us for a free reminder service. We can help you book your next test in advance.
Yes! We offer same-day MOT appointments subject to availability. Call us early in the morning for the best chance of securing a slot. Emergency MOTs are also available for urgent situations.
